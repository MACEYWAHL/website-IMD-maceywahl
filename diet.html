Breakdown of a Bunnies Diet

Every bunny breed is slightly different on the amount of food they are supposed to eat everyday, but many guidelines are based on bunny weight. The food types given however will be the same.

Pellets

Good timothy hay pellets should be a regular part of an adult rabbit's diet. High levels of fiber are found in timothy hay-based pellets, which rabbits need for better digestion. Feed no pellets containing corn, nuts, or seeds.

Hay

For rabbits to have good digestive tract mobility, they need to eat enough fiber. Timothy hay offers the satisfying nutritional benefits of protein along with a high fiber content. Timothy hay has shown to be an effective supporter of oral health due to the high fiber content in the stems. Rabbits teeth never stop growing over their lifetime so chewing is essential to health.

Fresh Vegetables

Vegetables should be given to your rabbit daily. Rabbits prefer green leafy items, although they can also consume herbs, root vegetables, and occasionally even small amounts of fruit.
