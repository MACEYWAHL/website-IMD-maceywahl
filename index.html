Many small animals get mistreated or are viewed as a toy or a gift, which they are indeed not, they are much more than that. Ranging from fish to bunnies, there is so much to learn about how to properly take care of them and bond with them. Animals bought on a whim or for a kid's birthday are very likely to get mistreated if the owners are not educated as to how to care for them and know their needs before making any purchases.

Before you adopt a furry friend, make sure you have these things to ensure a foundation for happiness for you and your bunny:

Plenty of time

Bunnies require daily maintenance and lots of time for bonding

Plenty of money to spend

If you can’t afford the vet, you can’t afford the pet

Plenty of patience

Bunnies are prey animals and behave much differently

A safe and calm environment

Bunnies are FRAGILE animals, not for young kids
